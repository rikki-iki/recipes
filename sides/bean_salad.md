# Bean salad

## Ingredients

* 1 can Black beans
* 1 can kidney beans
* 1 can butter beans
* 1 red onion
* 1 green capsicum
* 1/2 bunch parsley
* 1 tsp paprika
* 1 tbs white vinegar
* 1 tbs brown sugar
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 tbs extra virgin olive oil

## Method

Drain the beans and add to bowl. Finely slice the onion, capsicum and parsley and mix into the beans along with the remaining ingredients.
