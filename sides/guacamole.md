# Guacamole

## Ingredients

* 3 avocados
* ½ red onion
* 1 garlic clove
* 1 jalapeno
* 1 lime juice
* ½ bunch coriander
* Salt & pepper to taste

## Method

Mash the avocados into a bowl with the lime juice, salt and pepper.

Finely dice the onion, garlic, jalapeno and coriander and mix into the avocado so it's evenly combined.
