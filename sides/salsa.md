# Salsa

## Ingredients

* 3 good tomatoes
* 1 green capsicum
* 1/2 red onion
* 1/2 bunch coriander
* 1 lime juice
* 1 tbsp tabasco sauce
* Salt and pepper to taste

## Method

Finely slice tomatoes, capsicum, onion and coriander and combine in bowl.

Add lime juice, tabasco and season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
