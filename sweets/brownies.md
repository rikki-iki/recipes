# Brownies

## Ingredients

* 150g dark chocolate
* 1/2 caster
* 2/3 self raising
* 100g butter
* 3 eggs
* 60g walnuts
* 2 table water
* 100g dark chocolate extra

## Method

Line a baking tin.

Melt water, choc and butter.

Add sugar and cool leave to cool.

Sift in flour and eggs and stir.

Add extra chocolate and nuts.

Bake at 180c for 35 minutes.
